Welcome to the Culmer Family

This is the home of the Culmer family, and in this family there are quite a few surnames:

  • Culmer
  • Umstead
  • Petre
  • Campbell's
  • Wells
  • Newsome's
  • Cox's
  • Stansel's
  • White's

So far in my research of the Culmer family, I have found that we go back as far as the late 1600's to the early 1700's.
